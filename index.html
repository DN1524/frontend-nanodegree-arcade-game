SPEEDING BUGGERS

Gain as many points as possible within one minute and without losing all 3 lives to win!

water tiles = 50 points and the boat = 250 points!

If all lives are lost before one minute, you lose.

Tip: All the bugs' speeds and movement orientaions (moving left or right) are randomized, so be careful when trying to predict their behavior.

YOU LOSE!

You failed to live through the speeding buggers before the timer ran out!

YOU WIN!

You successfully lived through the speeding buggers within the given time!
